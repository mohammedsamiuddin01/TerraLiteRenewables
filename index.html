Meet Our Co-Founders

Arshad Syed

Co Founder

With a strong foundation in both engineering and business, I am proud to serve as Co-Founder of Terra Lite Renewables. My academic journey began with a B.Tech in Electrical and Electronics Engineering (EEE), followed by an M.Tech in Power Industrial Drives and an MBA in Marketing. This unique blend of technical and managerial expertise has shaped my vision for driving innovation in the renewable energy sector. Over the past nine years, I have dedicated my career to advancing solar and electric vehicle charging infrastructure across India. My professional journey started at Green Life Energy Solutions, where I served as a Project Engineer for three years, gaining invaluable hands-on experience in sustainable energy projects. I then joined Fourth Partner Energy Pvt. Ltd. as a Senior Site Manager, leading teams and executing projects for four years. Driven by a passion for entrepreneurship, I co-founded Anshu Power Projects, where I had the privilege of serving over 150 clients and delivering impactful solar solutions. My most recent role was as EVSE Manager at Lithium Urban Technologies, where I focused on expanding EV charging infrastructure for 1.5 years. At Terra Lite Renewables, I am channeling my experience and passion into developing cutting-edge solar and wind energy projects. My commitment is to accelerate the transition to greener, more sustainable energy solutions, empowering communities and businesses for a cleaner future. Let’s work together to build a brighter, sustainable tomorrow.
